About Save the Amazon Volunteering

Save the Amazon is an international volunteering initiative dedicated to protecting and preserving the Amazon rainforest, which is vital for global climate regulation and biodiversity conservation.

The Amazon rainforest is the largest tropical rainforest in the world and home to millions of plant and animal species. However, it is facing threats such as deforestation, illegal logging, and wildfires, which endanger its ecosystems and indigenous communities.

Volunteering with Save the Amazon offers opportunities to participate in various conservation activities, including reforestation efforts, wildlife monitoring, sustainable development projects, and community outreach programs.

By volunteering with Save the Amazon, individuals can make a direct impact on rainforest conservation while experiencing the unique biodiversity and culture of the Amazon region.
